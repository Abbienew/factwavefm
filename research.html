Research


Experiment Design

The radio station started from a conceptual idea and then moved on to comprehensive research. This research includes areas such as broadcast media, news research, the application of generative AI in journalism, and music and sound arrangement in broadcasting. After completing the research, I proceeded to prototype design and eventually produced the interactive installation you experience today.


How Does the Podcast Function?

According to 'Key Concepts in Radio Studies,' radio can consist of various elements such as Acoustics, Broadcast Talk, Comedy, DJs and Presenters, Documentaries and Features, Drama, Magazines, Music, Phone-ins, Podcasting, Recording, Serials and Soaps, Sport, and Talk Radio. The initial phase of the 'FactWave FM' project explores the Internet format, focusing on Podcasting and music components, which can be experienced through an interactive installation. The next stage that 'FactWave FM' is interested in experimenting with is Phone-ins. If you're interested in learning more, please feel free to contact 'FactWave FM'.


News Reference

FactWave FM is an unfunded experiment where various types of AI-generated broadcast news are adapted from current mainstream media publications. Therefore, if any news content infringes on copyrights, please inform the station, and I will take down the content.


Data Privacy

At FactWave FM, your privacy is our top priority. As part of our unfunded "Collective News Authenticity Perception" experiment, we collect minimal, anonymous interactive data strictly for research purposes. Your personal information is never shared and is securely stored in compliance with privacy laws. For more in-depth information, please read our full Privacy Policy.
